ULT Development

Ultimate attacks 'ULTs' are rewarded to a player after scoring three knockdowns with a ball. ULTs were designed to improve each characters weakest ability, and to encourage and reward players for actively seeking out knockdowns. Apart from the comical effect from the rag-dolling player and the goal to be last player standing. We felt there was no reward for the individual. Including a system which praises players involvement by offering a special ability drives the gameplay forward, and adds additional emphasis on a more direct style of gameplay.
