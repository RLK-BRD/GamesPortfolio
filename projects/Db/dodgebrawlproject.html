DODGEBRAWL

Project Description

​ DodgeBrawl takes the classic real-world game of dodgeball and turns it up to eleven, delivering a fast-paced, chaotic, and highly entertaining experience. Players will need quick reflexes, clever tactics, and to embrace the madness as they battle it out and eliminate opponents in spectacular and hilarious ways.

Featuring multiple dodgeball-inspired game modes, DodgeBrawl offers everything from traditional elimination matches to King of the Hill and a variety of unique twists on the formula. With environmental hazards that can be triggered by players or occur as random events, no two matches play out the same way. Whether competing against friends or teaming up in local couch co-op, players can expect hours of unpredictable fun and intense dodgeball action.

DodgeBrawl was my third year University project, which got selected for Transfuzer and was showcased at EGX, resulting in it winning the public vote for the favourite game out of the Transfuzer catergory.

My Role

My role on this project consisted of Game Designer and developing mechanics via Unreal Blueprints. I would frequently test the project, tweaking player movement and throw speed, as well as balancing different stats for characters.

I developed the ability for the players to move, pick up a ball, throw (with a charge function), dash/barge, special abilities (ults), and catching a ball which gives the player a instant full power throw as a risk/reward bonus. I was also in charge of creating the menu functionality.
