About Me

I have over seven years of experience designing and developing engaging, gamified applications and experiences. In my time I have been fortunate to work on various projects, from games to training simulations built for multiple platforms ranging from PC to Android.

As a naturally inquisitive person, I love to discuss ideas with other creative individuals. I am constantly seeking new ways to challenge myself and push the boundaries of what is possible. Whether it's through my work or my personal pursuits, I am always striving to learn, grow, and achieve my goals.

During my career, I've gained a unique perspective on the impact that games and gaming technology can have on the end user. In particular, a VR therapy tool has been designed to alleviate anxiety in patients undergoing MRI scans. Being part of this project has given me a deeper appreciation for the potential of gaming technology to make a positive impact on people's lives.
